FAQ

What is Unified Trading Terminal?
The Unified Trading Terminal enables you to trade on GMX or GNS without having to switch between interfaces.
Does DeDeLend take any fees?
We don't charge any fees, so you'll pay the same fees as if you were trading on the original GMX or GNS interfaces.
Is it safe?
We don't use any contracts. You interact directly with the contracts of GMX and GNS. We simply provide an interface between you and the blockchain world.
What if I have a problem with Unified Trading Terminal?
If you experience any issues with the Unified Trading Terminal, you can always manage your positions using the original GMX or GNS interfaces.
